Project 3: Thesis and Summary

Our team sought to link the salaries earned by high school and college graduates to see if there is a predictor to the type of degree you earn and the salary you earn. We looked at a 2016 survey called the National Education and Attainment Survey (NEAS). Plugging the results into a predictive decision tree, we were able to determine the leading factors to the salaries people earn post degree. This linked into our second project by looking at how the rising college debt compares to people's ability to earn enough to cover the margin.
